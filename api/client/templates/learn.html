How does QuarkyAI work?

Step 1
QuarkyAI is a chatbot that uses natural language processing to understand and respond to user input. It is designed to provide information and assistance on a wide range of topics. QuarkyAI utilizes a free, pretrained model 'llama-3.3-70b-versatile'
Step 2
Once a user gives an input, QuarkyAI processes the input and generates a reasoning for the response. The reasonsing is meant to provide a better understanding of how the chatbot can get to an almost certain response.
Step 3
Once QuarkyAI has finished reasoning, it will provide a response to the user. The response is generated based on the reasoning and the input given by the user.
